Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-06

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data Match FEAR (“the Game”) processes and why.

What we process

When you sign in, the Game uses Epic Online Services (EOS) to authenticate you for leaderboards. The data we collect is limited to what is necessary for providing the game’s services.

How we use data

The Game uses player data only for leaderboards and friends leaderboards. Future iterations of the Game might use player data for achievement tracking or in-game rewards.

Data sharing

We share data only with service providers needed to run the Game (for example, Epic Online Services for auth and leaderboards). We do not sell or share player data with third parties, except as required by law or as necessary to comply with legal process.

Your choices

Usage of leaderboards is completely optional. You can sign out at any time from the in-game menu.

Epic Games Privacy Policy

In addition to this privacy policy, the use of EOS is also subject to Epic Games’ privacy policy, which can be found at https://www.epicgames.com/site/en-US/privacypolicy.

Changes

We reserve the right to change this privacy policy at any time without prior notice. Any changes will be posted on our website and will become effective immediately upon posting.

If you have questions, contact us at RadiantWolf Games.
